Orisa

Tips

When being flanked, try using your Halt! ability to pull the flanker into a predictable spot

Place high priority on protecting your Supercharger after you’ve placed it. 50% damage increase for everyone in range is enough to turn the tides of a team fight.

If your team has a Roadhog, try coorinating a Halt! Hook combo. This is a great way to get a pick from behind enemy shields.

Try not to waste too many shots from your primary fire chasing fast squishies. Instead, try pouring it into your enemy’s frontline, as it can be deasting to shields and other tanks.

Take care with your Fortify ability, 50% damage reduction is enough to survive many damage ults and completely protects you from ults like Earthshatter and Graviton Surge.

Strategy

Orisa seems like a straightforward front line tank, but she has a lot of nuance and a plethora of abilities that makes her one of the most difficult tanks to master.

Like most tanks, Orisa’s primary objective is to create space for her team, she does this primarily by progressively moving up her shield and laying suppressive fire.

Orisa is best when run with a slower death-ball comp where you make more gradual pushes. Alternatively, Lucio’s speed boost is an effective way to make her work with faster comps.

Counters/weaknesses

While strong in many situations, Orisa can still have trouble when heroes can get around her extensive defenses

Reaper: If Orisa has limited team support, Reaper will have an easy time getting behind her and making quick work of her due to Orisa’s limited dps output.

Widowmaker/Hanzo: Orisa has one of the largest heads in the game and moves slowly and predictably, making her an easy target for even a decent sniper if exposed

Widowmaker

Tips

If you are having trouble aiming, try lowering your sensitivity.

Scoped shots use 3 bullets regardless of charge, so do your best to hold your fire until charged if possible.

If squishy heroes are behind Rein shield, wait for him to drop his shield to take your shot. People often feel safer than they are.

Nothing else matters if you miss your shots but if your placement and positioning are bad, you may never get the chance.

Shoot other shields, they are more fragile then they appear.

If you are on high ground and get jumped on by Winston or Dva, jump off and bait them into following you, if they chase you you can hook back to safety and leave them with no movement abilities.

Strategy

In the hands of someone skilled, Widowmaker can quickly become one of the strongest carry heroes in the game.

While she is a sniper, your aim is not everything with Widow. Positioning and spacing are arguably more important.

Widow is viable in most team comps, but if the enemy is running a dive comp, Widow may struggle to get her footing.

Counters/weaknesses

A good Widowmaker can be countered in one of two ways, a better Widowmaker, or invading her space.

Dva/Winston: both of these dive tanks excel in getting in your face and messing with you. They may have a hard time killing her, due to her hook, but they can make it very difficult for her to find footing.

Symmetra: her ultimate can basically render Widow worthless for it’s duration if placed well.
