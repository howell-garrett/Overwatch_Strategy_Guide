Orisa

Tips

When being flanked, try using your Halt! ability to pull the flanker into a predictable spot

Place high priority on protecting your Supercharger after you’ve placed it. 50% damage increase for everyone in range is enough to turn the tides of a team fight.

If your team has a Roadhog, try coorinating a Halt! Hook combo. This is a great way to get a pick from behind enemy shields.

Try not to waste too many shots from your primary fire chasing fast squishies. Instead, try pouring it into your enemy’s frontline, as it can be deasting to shields and other tanks.

Take care with your Fortify ability, 50% damage reduction is enough to survive many damage ults and completely protects you from ults like Earthshatter and Graviton Surge.

Strategy

Orisa seems like a straightforward front line tank, but she has a lot of nuance and a plethora of abilities that makes her one of the most difficult tanks to master.

Like most tanks, Orisa’s primary objective is to create space for her team, she does this primarily by progressively moving up her shield and laying suppressive fire.

Orisa is best when run with a slower death-ball comp where you make more gradual pushes. Alternatively, Lucio’s speed boost is an effective way to make her work with faster comps.

Counters/weaknesses

Reaper: If Orisa has limited team support, Reaper will have an easy time getting behind her and making quick work of her due to Orisa’s limited dps output.

Widowmaker/Hanzo: Orisa has one of the largest heads in the game and moves slowly and predictably, making her an easy target for even a decent sniper if exposed

Reinhardt

Tips

When you have earthshatter try to keep try of the enemy team’s abilities and shields that can potentially block it.

The number 1 rule of your Charge ability, mind where you’ll land. You typically want to charge where you’ll stop close to your team. If you charge an enemy past their backline, there is little chance you survive that engagement.

Use your Fire Strike liberally in order to gain ultimate charge, especially early in a match.

Strategy

Always be managing your shield health, without it you are a bit of a sitting duck.

Do this by optimizing the places you set up, try to use surroundings to help you tank for your team, the payload has infinite health so try to set up behind it.

Third person perspective give you a POV advantage over most, use this to call shots for your team.

Counters/weaknesses

Bastion: Be conscious of where Bastion is set us as he can shred your shield and you. Resist the urge to charge him unless you have healing support from an Ana and/or a Zarya bubble..

Tracer:Tracer can circumvent your greatest asset, your shield, if she has ult she can one clip bomb you and you’re dead in an instant. Make sure you are tracking her and communicating your location as there is little you can do to stop her.

Roadhog

Tips

Try using Whole Hog as a way to keep enemies off point and stopping overtime.

Alternatively, try using Whole Hog to push big enemies into a corner and do much bigger damage.

You can turn about 90 degrees after hooking someone and they will land there. You can use this to get environmental kills.

Hook is one of the best answers to bastion. Not only does it move him, but it takes him out of turret form.

Primary fire should only be used in very close range situations, otherwise secondary is usually more effective.

Strategy

Despite his label, Roadhog is not really a tank, at least not in the way the others are. Unlike all other tanks, Roadhog has no ability to absorb or prevent damage. Instead, Roadhog is much more of a damage dealer.

Since his damage nerf many heroes can now escape his hookshot combo alive. Roadhog’s primary job now is to hook enemies into the team, as junkrat says “you hook em, I’ll cook em”

If not hooking enemies, Roadhog’s next strongest asset is his secondary fire. Use your increased fire rate to launch shrapnel at the enemies frontline.

Counters/weaknesses

Reaper: Especially since the nerf, Roadhog struggles to take reaper out in one shot which gives him the ability to have a field day with his big damage output and passive healing.

Ana: Roadhog’s healing ability allows him to get away with taking big damage, but the startup on the ability makes him an easy target for an Ana Biotic Grenade.

Winston

Tips

Don’t use your bubble like a Rein or Orisa shield. That isn’t what it’s for. Instead, try using it on the objective in the middle of a team fight for the ability to weave in and out of enemies range.

Winston’s primary fire will chain through enemies if they’re close enough, so try to engage in large team fights.

When you dive your targets, try to land on or near them because the landing does 45 damage which can really help you rush them down.

Be careful when jumping on Hanzo, with the recent updates Hanzo’s Storm Arrow allows him to shred some tanks.

If you put your bubble over Dva’s ultimate it will destroy the shield and cancel the ult, no matter how close the shield is to being broken.

Strategy

Winston is a mobility dive tank to his core. With a 6 second cool down on your Jump Pack you always need to be moving around.

The short range of his primary fire further exemplifies this. Winston needs to be right in the middle of the action in order to be effective.

Winston’s mobility makes him very capable of dealing with backline heroes like Widowmaker and is expected to do so.

Counters/weaknesses

Reaper: Chasing down a Reaper as Winston is a fool’s errand, he does more damage and has passive healing and at full health even the worst Reaper players can take Winston down.

Mei: Winston’s need to be at close range also makes him susceptible to Mei’s freeze. Mei can freeze Winston and get a quick headshot before Winston can even do much of any damage.

Widowmaker

Tips

If you are having trouble aiming, try lowering your sensitivity.

Scoped shots use 3 bullets regardless of charge, so do your best to hold your fire until charged if possible.

If squishy heroes are behind Rein shield, wait for him to drop his shield to take your shot. People often feel safer than they are.

Nothing else matters if you miss your shots but if your placement and positioning are bad, you may never get the chance.

Shoot other shields, they are more fragile then they appear.

If you are on high ground and get jumped on by Winston or Dva, jump off and bait them into following you, if they chase you you can hook back to safety and leave them with no movement abilities.

Strategy

In the hands of someone skilled, Widowmaker can quickly become one of the strongest carry heroes in the game.

While she is a sniper, your aim is not everything with Widow. Positioning and spacing are arguably more important.

Widow is viable in most team comps, but if the enemy is running a dive comp, Widow may struggle to get her footing.

Counters/weaknesses

A good Widowmaker can be countered in one of two ways, a better Widowmaker, or invading her space.

Dva/Winston: both of these dive tanks excel in getting in your face and messing with you. They may have a hard time killing her, due to her hook, but they can make it very difficult for her to find footing.

Symmetra: her ultimate can basically render Widow worthless for it’s duration if placed well.
